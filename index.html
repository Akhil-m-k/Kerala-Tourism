Experience Kerala

AYURVEDA

Ayurveda is the oldest healthcare system in the world. This traditional healthcare system combines the profound thoughts of medicine and philosophy.

KERALA CUISINE

Kerala is very food-lover's paradise. Kerala cuisine offers a multitude of dishes,ranging from the traditional sadya to seafood delicacies.Both vegitarians and non-vegetariants have a wide variety of dishes to choose from.

VILLAGE LIFE

Village life of God's on country is a symphony composed on strings of bueaty painted with myriad traditions and cultures

FESTIVALS

The festival season, the pride and joy of kerala, is epitomised by the ferocious beats of the chenda (drums) emanating across the landscape

ARTFORMS

Kerala has birthed and nurtured a plethora of art forms over a thousand years. Our shores have watched them evolve and flourish in various shapes and forms.

BACKWATERS

The backwater regions of Kerala are one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world. The tranquil backwater cruises are a once in a lifetime experience
